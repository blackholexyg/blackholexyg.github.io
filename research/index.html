About

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering Department at Northwestern University. I work with Professor Yonggang Huang on mechanics modeling and design of stretchable electronics and microfluidic devices. I have published more than 20 papers in journals, including sister journals of Science and Nature, and top mechanics journal JMPS. I was invited as reviewer for Journal of Applied Mechanics (Transactions of the ASME).
